Page Instructions

The Rule Set allows the modification and synchronization of Suricata rules on a per sensor basis. Suricata rules can be enabled / disabled and tested against existing PCAP files.

Prior to clicking the "Rule Sync" button in the top right, ensure sensors are assigned to the rule set by clicking the “Edit” button and selecting the required sensors to apply.

After clicking the "Rule Sync" button, ensure the state column displays "<sensor_name> - Synced"

For amplifying information, please refer to the Policy management – Rule Sync section of the DIP Operations guide.
